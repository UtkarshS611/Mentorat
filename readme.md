# MentoRat
## A student mentor connection platform
[![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/joemccann/dillinger.svg?branch=master)](https://travis-ci.org/joemccann/dillinger)

Connecting students and teachers collaboratively
Made with HTML5, CSS3 , JAVASCRIPT




## Features

- Slot Booking: Students can easily book mentorship sessions with teachers based on their availability, ensuring a seamless scheduling experience.
- Teacher Profiles: Detailed information about teachers, including their qualifications, expertise, and areas of interest, helping students choose the right mentor.
- Community Forum: A space for students and teachers to engage in discussions, share resources, and support each other's learning journeys.
- Contact Form: A straightforward way for users to reach out with inquiries, feedback, or support requests, enhancing communication.
- Automated Emails: Notifications and reminders for upcoming sessions, new messages, and community updates to keep users informed and
